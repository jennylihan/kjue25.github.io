Fun Facts:

  • Cacti use there spines to store water and survive in the desert. If fog lands on there spines, it then turns into water and drips down on the groud below it. After that, the cacti roots absorbs the water and keep it stored. A cactus stores gallons of water in just it's stem!
  • There are around 1,600 different species of cacti in the world!
  • Dragon Fruit is part of the cactus family!
  • Cacti come in all shapes and sizes!

Oyster Mushroom

By Eve D.

The Oyster mushroom is an edible mushroom that is commonly found in sub-tropical and temperate climates.

The Oyster Mushroom is a popular mushroom to consume, especially for its taste and texture. The Oyster Mushroom has been proven to have many essential nutrients.

How to grow oyster mushrooms.

Some fun facts about the oyster mushroom:

    -The oyster mushroom caps usually range from 5-25

    -They can be tan, yellow, brown, white, and even sometimes pink!

    -The oyster mushroom is one of the most cultivated mushrooms in the world

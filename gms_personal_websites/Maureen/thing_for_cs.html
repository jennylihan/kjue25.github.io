Naegleria fowleri

By Maureen S.


Naegleria fowleri (Ameoba) causes the disease primary amebic meningoencephalitis (PAM), a brain infection that leads to the destruction of brain tissue. Known simply as the "brain eating ameoba" it is a species of the genus Naegleria. Naegleria fowleri is not classified as a true ameoba, but a shape shifting amoeboflagellate excavate.

Vaquita

Scientific Name: Phocoena sinus


Kingdom: Animalia
Phylum: Chordata
Class: Mammalia
Order: Artiodactlya
Family: Phocoenidae
Genus: Phocoena
Species: Phocoena sinus

The vaquita is an endangered species of porpoise. Its name translates to "Little Cow."
It is currently the rarest marine mammal, and it is estimated that there are only about 19 left in the world.
Vaquita are usually around 3.9 - 4.9 feet long as an adult.
The biggest threat to vaquita are fishing nets, most of which are meant to catch other animals, such as totoba fish,
but often end up inadvertently catching vaquita.

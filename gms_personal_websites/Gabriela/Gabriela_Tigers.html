What is a tiger?

Tigers are a cat, a big preditorial cat. Tigers are carnivors wich means they only eat meat.

Tigers are also smart. There are multiple kinds of tigers, there are Bengal tigers that are black

and orange stripped, and Siberian tigers that are white and black stripped, just to name a few.

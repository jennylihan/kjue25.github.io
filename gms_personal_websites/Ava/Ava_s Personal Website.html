The scientific name for everything is the genus and the species. The scientific name for the camellia flower is camellia japonica.

Every plant has a taxonomic classification! This is the classification for the camellia flower.

A more common name for this flower is Japanese Camellia, or tsubaki in Japanese. It is an evergreen shrub that typically grows from 6 to 12 feet. The Japanese Camellia is native to Japan and China. These flowers are cultivated because of their leaves, which are used for the making of tea, and the flowers, which are used for decorative purposes.

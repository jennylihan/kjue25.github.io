Art cards are sometimes made in color, while others are just in plain Black and White. This is the Black and White zentangle I did:

I was inspired by flowers, which is why there is a kind of flower vibe. Click HERE for a website to show you how to create your own zentangle!!!

Zentangles can be animals, people, objects, flowers, and more. Zentangle is a good way to just calm yourself. It helps sooth your brain after an intense day at work or school. I highly suggest to start doing zentangles for fun if you are highly stressed a lot. Zentangles are a good way to use your free time because there is no limit to what you can make.

About my art zentangle

This is my art card, my art card is a zentangle. Drawing zentangles are used to relax and calm down. How you draw is by getting a sharpie and drawing doodles inside of lines.

Why I choose zentangle

They are very relaxing to draw. They can help you calm down by drawing them and looking at them! I thought people would like getting a zentangle on their holliday cards or magnets.

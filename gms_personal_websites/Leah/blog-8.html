Leaf Sheep

A Informational Site by Yours Truely

Leaf Sheep, are very interesting animals. Leaf sheep are actually sea slugs, that look like they are sheep with leaves on their backs. They can be found near the Philippines, Japan and Indonesia. Their latin name is Costasiella Kuroshimae. They belong to the Animalia kingdom, their phylum is Mollusca. The Leaf Sheep's class is Gastropoda, they belong to the Sacoglossa order and species. Leaf Sheep are in the Costasiellidae family and genus.

hedgehogs as pets:

hedgehogs are common pets in many areas. the care involved is similar to a hamster because they need a cage and hamsters and hedgehogs are close in size. Although they are fun pets to have, they are not legal in many states in the US (california, georgia, hawaii, etc.), and in some other states they are legal but you need a permit to own one as a pet (arizna, maine, wyoming, etc.)

Zombie Survival Tips

By Claire B.

Tip 1: get supplies

Collect supplies! You will need: Non perishable food Drinking water Sleeping bag (or warm clothes) Weapons (long distance and for hand to hand combat, preferably silent) A backpack A flashlight with batteries

Tip 2: make a plan

You will need a plan. Figure out a location that is remote enough that people won't be flocking there, but not so remote that if you have to start scavenging, you won't be able to find civilization.

Tip 3: destroy the brain

To kill a zombie you need to destroy the brain, whether that be through stabbing the head or just using a general firearm. After you kill it, to dispose of the body you should probably burn it.

Kangaroos

I'm going to focus on the Eastern Grey Kangaroo the scientific name for the Eastern Grey Kangaroo is Macropus giganteus.
The scientific classification for the Eastern Grey Kangaroo is
Kingdom: Anamalia
Phylum: Cordata
Class: Mamalia
Infraclass: Marsupialia
Order: Diprotodontia
Family: Macropodidae
Genus: Macropus
Species: M. giganteus

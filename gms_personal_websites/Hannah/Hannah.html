Common Sunflower

by Hannah R.

Common Sunflowers scientific name Is: Helianthus Annuus.

  • Family: Asteraceae

  • Kingdom: Plantae

  • Clade: Tracheophyta

  • Clade: Angiosperms

  • Clade: Eudicots

  • Clade: Asterids

  • Order: Asterales

  • Genus: Helianthus

Relatives of the same species are: The Swamp Sunflower and The Western Sunflower

Fun Fact: The world's tallest sunflower reaches 30 feet and 1 inch.

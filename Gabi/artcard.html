GABI'S ART CARD!

By: Gabi R.

I really enjoyed the process of creating this art card, one of my favorite parts of it was that if I made a "mistake" or I did something to the drawing that I maybe didn't like to much, I couldn't go back and fix it because we were instructed to use black pen only. I just had to accept that it was okay or I could find a way to fix it so that I liked it better. The process of the actual design wasn't too interesting, my original idea was to make an abstract portrait:

(Click here to read and learn more about abstract art)

I wasn't to fond of the idea so I kept on brainstorming, I made little doodles and tried to think of anything. The idea of my finished product kind of just came out of nowhere. I don't relly know where I got my inspiration but bassicly I drew a big eye in the middle of the paper

Even though the design is pretty simple and it doesn't have much details, I enjoyed coloring in the empty spaces. It was pretty relaxing (although the sharpie fumes were pretty bad). Overall the process/unit of the art card was pretty fun! :)

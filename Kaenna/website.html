House Mice

 

An Informational Website by Kaenna


House mice are very common near where humans live. Their scientific name is Mus musculus. They're similar to other mice in the genus Mus, and one of the most abundant out of them. Mus musculus' full scientific name is:

Kingdom: Animalia
Phylum: Chordata
Class: Sarcopterygii
Order: Rodentia
Family: Muridae
Genus: Mus
Species: Mus musculus

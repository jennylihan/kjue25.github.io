Zebras

By: Patrice

Zebras. They are fast moving striped animals. They can run on average 40 MPH and are omnivores. They mainly eat grass, but they can also eat leaves, shrubs, and even sometimes bark! A cool fact about zebras is that they cannot be domesticated or ridden like horses.

There taxonomic classifications are:

Kingdom-Animalia
Phylum-Chordata
Class-Mammalia
Order-Perissodactyla
Family- Equidae
Genus-Equus
Species-Equus quagga (plains zebra)

Plains zebras live in grasslands with no trees or wood. They are 3.6 to 4.8 feet (1.1 to 1.5 m) at the shoulder and weigh up to 770 lbs. (350 kg). Zebras are awesome creatures, if you want to learn in even more detail about them click here , which is where I got a lot of my information.

FAQ

What is the Acadamy of the Gifted?

The Acadamy of the Gifted is a school for those with telekenisis. It was founded in the year 3147 as a way for those with the power of telekinisis to learn and control it.

Why have I never heard of The Acadamy of the Gifted?

The Acadamy of the Gifted is a secret school. This means that no one not even your family can know about it. If any one found out the The Acadamy of the Gifted would have to close. It was founded as a way for those with telekenisis to learn to control their power. The governtment thinks that not having a school for telekenisis will make shure that no onw learns how to use it and no one will turn out like Robert Londols. In truth it is nesacery that you learn to control your power. The Government is jeuslos of us.

How was I accepted into The Acadamy of the Gifted? I never took a test or anything that I know of.

There is actually a test that everyone takes at the end of 5th grade. This is when telekinetic powers first start to develop. If you pass the test the you are admitted into The Acadamy of the Gifted. If you fail the test then you go to normal schools.

Once I passed the test I was sent to have a private talk with the janitor. Why?

The Scociety of Telekenisis has a member at every school. They can be the priciple, a teacher, the secretary or even the janitor. The members at the schools have a form of telekinisis that allows them to detect other people with the power of telekenisis.

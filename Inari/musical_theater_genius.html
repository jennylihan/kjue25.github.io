If you don't know, Hamilton is about Alexander Hamilton from American History and put into a musical! The whole thing is all songs, no dialogue except in the middle of a song.

The soundtrack is amazing and most of it is historically accurate! I would've never thought to make a musical about American history! You can learn a lot from Hamilton too.

Like I said, it follows Alexander Hamilton from the start of the American revoloution to his death. Aaron Burr is also a main character, and you see a lot of others too!

Fun Facts:

Bullet Character
  • Watch this video explaining "The Bullet". If you don't want to, here is a short summary: Basically there is this special ensemble member who appears right before someone dies. They foreshadow someone's death.
  • Alexander Hamilton (in the show) walks in curves and Burr walks in straight lines. Why is this? You tell me.
  • You know how the stage turns? Well, in each scene when it turns, it means something. If the turn tables turn left, it is going backwards in time. When it goes right, it is moving forwards.
